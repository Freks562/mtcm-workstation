# City of St. Petersburg AI RFI — Internal Review Checklist

Use this checklist before final submission packaging.

## Content quality

- [ ] Response is clear, practical, and human-in-control.
- [ ] Workload-relief and service-quality outcomes are described without replacement framing.
- [ ] No invented certifications, approvals, or past performance claims.
- [ ] Claims are evidence-based and consistent across all package documents.

## Compliance quality

- [ ] Governance/security/public-records sections are complete and internally consistent.
- [ ] Assumptions and constraints are explicitly stated.
- [ ] Open questions are logged and tracked with owners.
- [ ] Risks are documented with mitigation actions.

## Submission readiness

- [ ] Required attachments checklist is complete.
- [ ] Official RFI/addenda version used is documented.
- [ ] Formatting/page-limit/submission-method requirements are met.
- [ ] Internal approvers have signed off on final package language.

## Repo hygiene

- [ ] Changes are docs-only.
- [ ] No route/UI/runtime/Supabase changes included.
- [ ] Opportunities index links to current package paths.
